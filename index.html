Project Idea

Overview

The thing I have always been the most passionate about is my animals, and I have had many different types of animals over the years, often many at a time, so much that it was difficult to keep track of all their needs. I have tried calendars, reminders and even a few smartphone applications made for the purpose of tracking your pets health, however I found that all were lacking something and were too clunky or too difficult to use. But what if there was a smartphone application that could assist you in managing all your pets needs? Well that’s what I aim to do.

Motivation

My motivation for this project is simply to make parts of an animal’s owners life less stressful. With this application there would be no need to stress over when your animals’ health or managing an animal’s needs. This application would serve useful not just for the everyday pet owner but also for people with large properties with dozens or hundreds of animals. As of 2017 more than 62% of Australian households have pets, and with there being more than 85, 000 farms Australia wide there is certainly a market for the technology to better manage our furry friends.

Description

The smartphone application I am looking to design has a range of features, it will include 5 tabs as shown in the concept art, which would be designed to provide the following features.

Animals

This tab is to log each animals individual profile, including; breed, weight, dietary restrictions, injection dates, vet check ups dates and any other medical or non medical related issues that need monitoring. As this is designed not just for regular pet owners but also for any large scale farming, there is also the option to add animals as a larger quantity, such as a heard of sheep, that would require their sheering or medical check ups to be done at the same time.

Calendar

The calendar tab is where all created events/ reminders will appear in an easy to view layout, appearing just as a regular calendar, with the ability to categorize each animal/ or group of animals by colours for easy viewing.

Events

The events tab is where to go to add an upcoming event, such as a vet appointment. This will have the ability to set reminders to warn of upcoming events, to add notes specific to each event, as well as input the location for each event, with the option to remind at location.

Manage

The manage tab has the ability to store all necessary data on any service/ product related to our pets lives, including veterinary records (eg medical records, vaccination dates). Such as adding contacts, like vets, dog groomers, or a daycare. There will also be the option to add specific foods, for example if your cat has special diet restrictions meaning they need to eat a certain brand of pet food. Toys or other products can also be added here, as recommended care information about your animals/ breeds that would be beneficial to managing them.

Care

The care tab would be used to provide recommended care advice for profiled pets/ animals, which would be catered to the species and breed of animal so the best possible advice could be provided. Some examples of recommended care would be diet, ideal weights, average exercise per day, training assistance and Frequently asked questions (to assist in answering the most common queries among todays pet owners).

While I do believe that there is a market for this type of application I can also recognize that there would be difficulties to face rolling it out. As I mentioned previously there are other smartphone applications out there that have some features of the same kind, and while none exist will the total package of features and usability there would still be competition in the market due to other versions being available for use. Another potential hurdle would be sourcing care recommendations for specific animals/ breeds, most importantly animals I have no experience with. An agreement would have to be reached with an organization or company that was able to provide, and continue to update this information, so that the user would be able to gain the most out of the applications services.
